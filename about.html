Our Story

25+ years of passion for motorcycles

A Legacy of Excellence

For over 25 years, YAMAHA Motodesign has been Corinth's trusted destination for premium motorcycles and scooters. What started as a small family business in 1998 has grown into the region's leading motorcycle dealership, serving thousands of satisfied customers throughout Central Greece.

Our commitment to excellence, customer service, and genuine passion for two-wheeled vehicles has made us the go-to choice for motorcycle enthusiasts. As an official YAMAHA dealer, we offer the full range of YAMAHA motorcycles, scooters, and ATVs, backed by manufacturer warranties and expert support.

Whether you're a first-time rider or an experienced motorcyclist, our knowledgeable team is here to help you find the perfect bike that matches your needs, style, and budget. We believe that everyone deserves to experience the freedom and joy of riding.
